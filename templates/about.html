About Campus-Connect

Campus-Connect is a student-built community platform designed to centralize campus resources, events, and civic engagement opportunities. Instead of searching across multiple websites, emails, and group chats, students can use this single hub to discover free resources, browse campus events, and connect with their community.

What You Can Do on Campus-Connect

  • Find or share free community resources (food support, housing, technology, etc.)
  • Create, browse, and RSVP to campus and civic events
  • Comment on posts and join conversations
  • Verify helpful posts with upvotes

Getting Started

You can browse all resources and events without an account. To contribute or interact, you must log in.

Creating an Account

  • Go to Sign Up
  • Enter your name, email, password, and select your role
  • Once registered, you will be redirected to your welcome page

Logging In

  • Enter your email and password on the Login page
  • If your credentials are correct, you’ll be logged in and redirected
  • If not, you’ll be asked to try again

Using Resources

The Resources page lists all student-submitted community support options such as food assistance, technology help, housing support, professional development, and more.

Adding a Resource

  • Select Add New Resource
  • Fill in the required fields (title, category, description, status, etc.)
  • Click "Add Resource" to publish your resource

Editing or Deleting Resources

  • You may only edit or delete resources that you created
  • Use the “Edit” button to update, or the “Delete” button to remove

Using Events

Events include workshops, campus gatherings, civic engagement opportunities, and more. You can browse, filter by category, and RSVP to events.

Adding an Event

  • Select Add New Event
  • Enter event details such as time, location, category, and description
  • Click "Add Event" to publish it to the Events page

RSVP to an Event

  • Click View on any event
  • Select Yes or Maybe from the RSVP menu
  • See who else is attending

Editing or Deleting Events

  • You may only edit or delete events that you created

Calendar View

  • Click on calender view to see events laid out on an interactive monthly calendar for easier planning.

Comments & Community Interaction

  • Each resource and event has a comments section

Community Verification

Campus-Connect uses a simple upvote/downvote system to help students verify which resources are trustworthy and up-to-date.

  • Upvotes increase a resource’s credibility. Once a resource receives enough positive votes, it becomes marked as Verified by the Community.
  • Downvotes signal that something may be inaccurate, outdated, or misleading.
  • If a resource accumulates too many downvotes, its status changes to Needs Review or may be Removed from public view.

This voting system ensures that the platform stays reliable and student-driven. The community helps keep information accurate, safe, and useful for everyone.

Navigation & Search

  • Use the top navigation bar to switch between Resources, Events, Login, and Logout
  • Search by keyword or filter by category
  • Clear Filters resets the page to show all results

Thank you for using Campus-Connect! Our goal is to make campus support easier to find and community engagement more accessible.
